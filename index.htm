About Me

Hi, I’m Emmar Christian Perez, a Computer Engineering graduate from STI College Lipa. I’m passionate about technology, especially in the fields of software testing and cybersecurity.

During my OJT, I gained hands-on experience creating test scenarios, reporting bugs, and manually testing a real website. That helped me understand how to spot issues from a user’s point of view and sparked my curiosity about identifying basic website vulnerabilities.

I also have a solid understanding of computer hardware and enjoy exploring how systems work behind the scenes. After my OJT, I continued learning on my own through platforms like Udemy, diving into topics such as system protection, common threats, and basic security practices.

I’ve worked on projects involving automation, sensor-based systems, and quality assurance. I have experience in basic penetration testing, computer repair, and knowledge of Agile methodologies used in development teams.

I'm currently seeking an opportunity to start my career in entry-level software testing or cybersecurity, where I can apply my technical skills, attention to detail, and eagerness to learn. I’m motivated to grow professionally and contribute to building secure and reliable systems.
